Project overview

The project was based on the fact that the Design-agency Xperience AB had a request from Willys to create a persona based on their target group; Students. In order to become one of the leading foodchains Willys wanted to research their targetgroup and create a persona.

Work process

The work process was divided into 4 parts:

  • Interviews
  • Surveys
  • Analysis
  • Persona

    • Interviews: Learning about the User

    Interviews

    The goal with the interviews was to collect as much useful data as possible. The questions were designed to help us learn about the users goals, frustrations, habits and personalities. We recorded the interviews so that we could transcribe them later on.

    Guide and Questions

    Our interview guide was created in order for us to structure the questions under specific topics and for time estimation. We used qualitative questions in our interview script that was designed to give us information that we needed to know in order to create the persona. We had a total of 25 questions and 3 respondents. All of the questions were designed with the purpose of learning as much as possible about the users.

    Survey

    Survey

    We used Google Forms to create a quantitative survey and created questions that would tell us as much as possible about our persona. The survey had a total of 35 questions and we had 11 respondents.

    Analysis

    Analyzing the data

    After completing all of the interviews we transcribed the audio files. We then coded the text after the categorys:

  • Personality
  • Frustrations
  • Motivations
  • Goals
  • Habits
  • Plattforms

    • We took out the type value from the survey and compared it to the answeres we got from the interviews.
